The PM Story

Built by a PM, not an engineer

Second Brain didn't start with a wireframe or a Jira ticket. It started with a real problem and a conversation with Claude AI.

01 — The Problem

Two people, one very disorganised household

Tasks lost in WhatsApp threads. Ideas forgotten by morning. One person carrying the mental load while the other didn't even know there was a load to carry. We needed one shared brain.

02 — The Process

Product decisions, not code

Instead of briefing an engineer, I opened Claude AI and started describing what I needed in plain English — the UX, the data model, the invite flow, edge cases, and tradeoffs. Every feature was a product conversation.

03 — The Result

A real product, used by real people

Real-time sync, Google Sign-In, AI categorisation, due dates, notes, email invites, household insights, a weekly digest — shipped in weeks. No engineering team. No dev sprints. No compromise on product quality.

"The best PM skill isn't knowing how to code. It's knowing exactly what to build, who it's for, and how to communicate that clearly enough that the right tool can build it for you."
— Margi Desai, Product Manager & Founder of Second Brain
